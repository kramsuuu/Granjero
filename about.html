OUR STORY

Granjero didn’t start as a resort — it started as a family dream.
We began with simply building swimming pools. We realized that we can make something out of a place where people came to cool off, laugh, and spend time together. Over time, we realized we wanted to create something more; something quieter, warmer, and closer to nature. So we expanded, built slowly, and shaped this space with intention. When it came to choosing a name, we wanted it to mean something. Granjero is actually a combination of my siblings’ names, a little tribute to the people who inspired this place from the start. It’s personal, it’s ours, and now, we’re sharing it with you. Today, Granjero is more than a private resort. It’s an escape, a gentle breath of nature, a place to unwind, reconnect, and feel at peace. Every corner was designed with comfort in mind, every detail guided by the same love that built it.We hope you feel at home here, the same way our family always has.
