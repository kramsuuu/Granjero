RESORT & HOUSE RULES

Our resort & house rules are simple guidelines designed to ensure a safe, comfortable, and enjoyable stay for all our guests and to respect the resort's facilities. Please review them upon arrival.

No Smoking Indoors

  • A ₱500 cleaning fee will be charged for violations.
  • Please use designated smoking areas only
  • Dispose of cigarette butts properly

Save Energy

  • Turn off the air conditiner and lights when not in used.
  • Unplug electronic devices when not in use.
  • Report any electrical issues immediately.

Observe Quiet Hours

  • Keep noise levels down after 10PM.
  • Loud music, parties, or other guests shall keep noise levels to a minimum during these hours
  • Loud music and parties must end by 10:00 PM

Keep It Clean

  • Use the provided garbage bins to dispose of trash properly.

No Food or Drinks in the Pool Area

  • To maintain cleanliness and safety, food and drinks are not allowed near the pool.

Child Supervision

  • Children must be supervised by an adult at all times.

Swim at Your Own Risk

  • No lifeguard is on duty.

Behavior Policy

  • The resort reserves the right to refuse entry or remove anyone engaging in unruly or illegal behavior.

Guest Responsibility

  • Guests are responsible for their belongings. The management is not liable for lost or stolen items.

Handle Items with Care

  • Towels, bedsheets, blankets, and pillows are NOT free.
  • Any loss or damage will be charged accordingly.
  • Please report any damages immediately.
